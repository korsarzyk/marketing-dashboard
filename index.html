Creative Director · kreacje zoptymalizowane pod Meta Ads
🎥 Veo — Instagram Reel Ad (15s · 9:16)
Transform this interior design visualization into a cinematic 15-second Instagram Reel ad. Start with a slow push-in shot from the doorway. Warm golden-hour lighting (3200K). Subtle dust particles in sunlight. Smooth dolly movement revealing key design elements. End with 2-second hold on the hero piece. Aspect ratio 9:16 vertical. Style: luxury Architectural Digest. No text overlays. Cinematic depth of field. First 3 seconds must be visually striking without sound.
🖼️ Nano Banana 2 — Facebook Carousel (1:1)
Create a premium carousel ad image at 1080x1080px. Apply warm tone grading (+15 warmth, +10 contrast). Dark vignette 25% on edges. Leave 200px bottom strip with dark overlay for text. Add thin gold frame border (1.5px, #C9A96E). Style: luxury real estate magazine. Aspirational, exclusive. No generated text.
⚡ Veo — Instagram Stories Ad (9:16 · 10s)
Create a 5-10 second Instagram Stories ad. Full-screen 9:16. Fast smooth pan (1.5s), slow reveal of key feature (3s), fade to blurred background (2s for CTA overlay). Premium, not rushed. Warm cinematic grade. Leave top 15% and bottom 20% clear (UI safe zones).
